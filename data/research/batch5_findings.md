# Batch 5 Pet Research Findings

## Baby Carno
- **Element:** Not specified
- **Special Mechanics:** Assassin class bonus: +2% * class level damage for single target attacks in dungeons. Token improvement grants True Damage = ((Attack + Speed) / 3) * (0.5 * (1 + CL/50)). True Damage bypasses enemy defense, most effective early in fights before debuffs accumulate.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented for dungeons
- **Synergies:** None documented
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** None
- **Token Improvement:** Grants True Damage ability (bypasses defense). Very significant for dungeon combat.
- **Constraint Implications:** True Damage mechanic is unique and valuable early in fights. Assassin class is optimal for dungeon damage.

## Alien
- **Element:** Not specified
- **Special Mechanics:** Multiplies the chance an event happens in dungeons by 100% + (0.5% * class level), maxed at CL 50 for +25% event chance. Assassin class gives +0.85% * CL to single-target dungeon damage.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented
- **Synergies:** Works well in Water Temple D4 teams alongside Witch, Undine, Leviathan. Event chance multiplier benefits teams in Mountain D3 for god power and growth events.
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** None
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Dungeon event chance multiplier is a unique utility. Can be Supporter/Defender for event farming or Assassin for damage. Flexible role.

## Bat
- **Element:** Not specified
- **Special Mechanics:** Gains 40% extra class experience from all sources. Blacksmith specialist: +1% * CL equip creation speed/quality when class is Blacksmith. One of the best Blacksmiths for RTI infinity tab.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None for combat
- **Synergies:** Tied with Elemental, Elf, Dwarf as top Blacksmiths
- **Anti-synergies:** The 40% class XP bonus is DISABLED if any dungeon team member equips an Ego Sword or Gram (experience siphon weapons). Exception: solo Bat with Gram retains bonus.
- **Equipment Notes:** Cannot use Ego Sword/Gram on ANY team member without losing the class XP bonus
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Important team constraint: Ego Sword/Gram on any teammate disables Bat's class XP bonus. Planner should warn about this.

## Flying Eyeball
- **Element:** Not specified
- **Special Mechanics:** First attack MARKS the enemy with lowest max HP instead of dealing damage. All other pets then target the marked enemy. Marked enemies take (10 + CL/4)% extra damage, max 35% at CL 100. Marked enemies take 25% extra damage from Sniper specifically. If Flying Eyeball has 500+ Speed, subsequent actions after the first become normal attacks.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented
- **Synergies:** Strong synergy with Sniper (25% extra damage on marked targets). Speed-focused gear recommended so it marks before others attack.
- **Anti-synergies:** Ego Sword/Gram on any team member removes the bonus (same as Bat's restriction).
- **Equipment Notes:** Prioritize Speed to ensure marking occurs first. Needs 500+ speed for subsequent normal attacks.
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Key synergy pair with Sniper. Marking mechanic means Flying Eyeball should be fastest on team. Ego Sword/Gram restriction applies to whole team. Adventurer class gives double Dungeon Level XP.

## Leviathan
- **Element:** Water (+2000 Water element innately)
- **Special Mechanics:** Counter-attack when hit or protecting allies, dealing 10% of Leviathan's Max HP as damage. Immune to Poison and Lethal Poison traps (critical for Water Temple). Gains 6 base growth per Dungeon Room (360 per full dungeon).
- **Stat Modifiers:** HP has x3.0 multiplier (tripled) in dungeons. ALL other stats reduced by half in dungeons.
- **Synergies:** Strong synergy with Witch (boosts Water elements, up to 1.5x at CL 100 Witch).
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** Focus entirely on HP (Neutral Equipment + Blue Gems). Later, use Knives against Ancient Mimics.
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Unique stat profile (3x HP, 0.5x everything else) means equipment must focus purely on HP. Natural Defender with 65% protect chance (vs standard 60%). Water Temple specialist due to poison immunity. Witch is a key partner.

## Basilisk
- **Element:** Not specified (locked to Mage class)
- **Special Mechanics:** Cannot attack normally. Uses Poison Explosion (AoE): initial hit does (150% + 1.5% * CL) damage to ALL enemies and reduces their elements by 10%. DoT follows at (50% + 0.5% * CL) damage per turn with 0.8% element reduction per tick. DoT persists even after Basilisk dies. Does NOT work against reflect enemies. Always deals 6 damage to Cosmic Gnome.
- **Stat Modifiers:** 1.5% specialist bonus for RTI infinity Mage slot when evolved
- **Synergies:** Needs secondary damage dealers since DoT alone may not clear tough fights. Stacks with Tier 4 knife element reductions.
- **Anti-synergies:** Useless against reflect enemies
- **Equipment Notes:** None specified
- **Token Improvement:** Doubles initial explosion damage AND DoT damage. Adds secondary explosion on turn 40. Obtained from Forest D4-10.
- **Constraint Implications:** Cannot be class-changed (always Mage). AoE-only attacker. Anti-synergy with reflect enemies is important for dungeon planning. Token improvement is very significant (doubles all damage + turn 40 explosion).

## Cherub
- **Element:** Not specified
- **Special Mechanics:** Reduces speed damage all party members take by 0.5% * CL. Bow attack (when equipped with bow and NOT Mage): 0.5% * CL chance to charm and stun enemies for 3 actions. Stun does NOT work in boss rooms. Defender class gets +10% protect chance.
- **Stat Modifiers:** Increases damage taken by extra 2% * CL (max 50%) as Defender
- **Synergies:** None documented
- **Anti-synergies:** Mage class disables bow stun mechanic
- **Equipment Notes:** Needs Bow equipped (not Mage) for stun mechanic
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Bow + non-Mage class required for stun. Stun disabled in boss rooms. Defender variant is a strong tank with extra protect chance and damage absorption.

## Todlicher Loffel
- **Element:** Counts as ALL non-neutral elements for dungeon events, but attacks as Neutral
- **Special Mechanics:** Reduces target's Defense and total Elements by 0.125% per hit. Defense can't go below 0, elements capped at 50% of base. Stacks with knife reductions. Mage class: +1.19% * CL multi-target dungeon damage.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented
- **Synergies:** Multi-element classification lets it satisfy monotype conditions while exploiting weaknesses. Good against Earth enemies like Corrupt Fairy Queen.
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** None specified
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Unique "all elements" classification is very special for team composition. Defense/element shredding stacks with knives. Mage class preferred for AoE damage bonus.

## Sphinx
- **Element:** Not specified
- **Special Mechanics:** Grants dungeon XP bonus to ALL pets: (0.75 + growth/250,000)%, maxed at 3.75% at 750k growth. Passive team-wide utility.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None for combat
- **Synergies:** Benefits entire team passively
- **Anti-synergies:** None
- **Equipment Notes:** None
- **Token Improvement:** Adds (0.05 * CL)% maxed at CL 75 for +3.75% additional. Combined max is 7.5% dungeon XP to all pets.
- **Constraint Implications:** Pure utility pet for dungeon XP farming. No combat role. Token improvement doubles the XP bonus potential.

## Sniper
- **Element:** Not specified
- **Special Mechanics:** Gets only ONE action per turn regardless of speed. Always attacks LAST. All damage is TRIPLED (3x). No back-row damage penalty. Assassin class: +2% * CL single-target dungeon damage.
- **Stat Modifiers:** +30% Attack, -10% HP, -10% Defense, -10% Speed
- **Synergies:** Strong synergy with Flying Eyeball (25% extra damage on marked targets, on top of triple damage). Good for Mountain D4 (speed constraints align naturally). Good for Gram/Lernean Hydra fights.
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** None specified, but Attack focus is implied
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Fixed 1 action/turn and always-last mechanics are critical constraints. 3x damage makes it a premier single-target damage dealer. Pair with Flying Eyeball for maximum effect. Stat modifiers (+30% ATK, -10% HP/DEF/SPD) affect equipment optimization.

## White Tiger
- **Element:** Not specified
- **Special Mechanics:** Instant Kill: triggers when enemy HP < (CL * 0.15%) of max HP. Deals pure damage equal to remaining HP. In boss rooms, threshold reduced to (CL * 0.05%). Does NOT function when evolved as Mage. Kill-based damage multiplier: +0.0058% per kill, capped at 10k kills = 58% max. Beyond 10k: kills^0.05%.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None for combat stats
- **Synergies:** Assassin class: +1% * CL single-target dungeon damage
- **Anti-synergies:** Mage evolution disables instant kill. Lernean Hydra and Chicken fights disable damage multiplier. Chicken also disables instant kill.
- **Equipment Notes:** Evolution requires Hurricane Armor, Bow, and Ring equipped (not consumed)
- **Token Improvement:** Adds the instant kill ability
- **Constraint Implications:** Must NOT be Mage class or instant kill is disabled. Boss threshold is reduced. Some special fights disable mechanics entirely. Token improvement is essential (grants the instant kill).

## Mist Sphere
- **Element:** Not specified
- **Special Mechanics:** As Supporter: does NOT have Attack-based heal component, only percent-based healing plus shield generation. Shield = (40% + 0.25% * CL) of Attack-based heal value. Shields primary target + 2 random allies (secondary shields halved). Shield cap: 50% of pet's max HP. Never attacks normally (always heals/shields). Supporter class: +0.9% * CL dungeon heal power.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented
- **Synergies:** Shields protect against venom damage (Lernean Hydra Gram fight, D4 Water Temple). Useful in fights that punish attacks (reflect enemies, Neutral Tower).
- **Anti-synergies:** Overrides Gnome's party-wide shield on Mist Sphere's targeted pet
- **Equipment Notes:** None specified
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Unique shield mechanic replaces attack-based healing. Anti-synergy with Gnome's shields (override). Ideal for reflect/venom fights where attacking is penalized. Never attacks, pure support.

## Elemental (Pet)
- **Element:** Not specified
- **Special Mechanics:** Increases element stats of ALL pets in same team by 0.5% per CL (50% at CL 100). Blacksmith class: own damage increased by 15% per CL (1500% at CL 100).
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented
- **Synergies:** Buffs entire team's elements. One of top Blacksmiths for RTI infinity tab (alongside Bat, Elf, Dwarf).
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** None specified
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Team-wide element buffer. Blacksmith class unlocks massive self-damage bonus. Dual role: team support + personal DPS.

## Black Tortoise
- **Element:** Not specified
- **Special Mechanics:** Increases damage taken instead of party member by extra 1% * CL (max 50%). Defender class: +10% protect chance.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented for base stats
- **Synergies:** Tank-focused team compositions
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** None specified
- **Token Improvement:** When hit, reduces attacker's speed by 25% of Black Tortoise's Defense stat (enemy speed floor: 70%). In boss/tower fights, this is only 20% as effective (5% of Defense).
- **Constraint Implications:** Premier tank pet. Token improvement adds enemy speed debuff on being hit. Defender class strongly preferred. Defense stat matters for token ability.

## Azure Dragon
- **Element:** Not specified
- **Special Mechanics:** Supporter class: +1% * CL dungeon heal power. Token improvement: places healing tokens on healed pets that trigger on direct damage, restoring 30% of Azure Dragon's heal power. One token per pet, destroyed on death, doesn't trigger from defender damage-sharing or reflected damage.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented
- **Synergies:** None documented
- **Anti-synergies:** Healing tokens don't work with defender damage-sharing
- **Equipment Notes:** Evolution requires Forest Armor, Axe, and Bracelet equipped (not consumed)
- **Token Improvement:** Healing tokens (30% heal on direct damage). Very significant for sustained healing.
- **Constraint Implications:** Supporter class required for heal bonus. Token healing tokens have specific limitations (no defender sharing, no reflect). Pairs well with non-Defender damage-takers.

## Pets with No Special Dungeon Constraints

The following pets have no notable dungeon combat mechanics, special constraints, or team synergies worth tracking in a dungeon planner:

- **Pignata** - No dungeon mechanics. Resource generation pet (Rebirth Bacon). No class bonus for any class.
- **Meteor** - Campaign-only pet. No dungeon mechanics whatsoever. Adventurer class campaign bonus only.
- **Lizard/Zookeeper** - Campaign/food pet. No dungeon combat mechanics. Adventurer class campaign bonus.
- **Goblin** - Campaign bonus pet scaling with Uncompleted Challenges. No dungeon combat mechanics. Adventurer class.
- **Koi** - Fishing/campaign pet. No dungeon combat mechanics.
- **Decorator Crab** - Campaign/item-finding pet. Shiny Stones mechanic for item find chance. Adventurer class. No combat mechanics.
- **Dwarf** - Blacksmith crafting pet. No direct dungeon combat mechanics. One of the top Blacksmiths for RTI infinity tab.
- **Caterpillar** - Alchemist crafting pet. No dungeon combat mechanics.
- **Bunny Girl** - Alchemist crafting pet. No dungeon combat mechanics.
- **Shadow Clone** - Produces Shadow Clones in dungeons as Adventurer but this is a resource mechanic, not combat. No combat stats or synergies.
