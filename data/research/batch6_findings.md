# Batch 6 Pet Research Findings

## Vermilion Pheasant
- **Element:** Fire (requires Inferno Armor/Sword/Gloves for evolution)
- **Special Mechanics:** Increases speed damage dealt by all party members by (CL x 0.25%). Rogue class ability: +1% * CL item drop rate in dungeons.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented
- **Synergies:** None documented
- **Anti-synergies:** Does NOT work with Hourglass on the same team (as of Sep 2024, a bug logs the ability firing but it does not actually work).
- **Equipment Notes:** Fire element focus (needs Inferno set for evolution, not consumed)
- **Token Improvement:** Boosts speed damage of all party pets.
- **Constraint Implications:** Must be evolved as Rogue for ability to work. Cannot be on same team as Hourglass. Speed damage party buff is unique.

## Vampire
- **Element:** Fire (evolution requires Magic Fire Stone)
- **Special Mechanics:** If Alchemist class, has DOUBLE stats in dungeons and can gain class experience in dungeons. Immune to burn status effect (Burn trap). Collects monster blood when she kills a monster in dungeons (killing blow required). Can craft Blood Potions for extra growth (100 base growth per potion, limited to 10 potions per Dungeon Level).
- **Stat Modifiers:** Double all stats when Alchemist class (2x multiplier in dungeons)
- **Synergies:** Strong in Depth 4 Volcano teams alongside Succubus and Improved Ape as fire-elemental damage dealers.
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** Fire element focus for Volcano teams
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented on wiki
- **Constraint Implications:** Alchemist class gives massive 2x stat bonus in dungeons - this is a huge constraint that means Alchemist is almost always optimal. Burn immunity makes her ideal for Volcano D4. Monster blood farming mechanic requires killing blows.

## Strategist
- **Element:** Not specified
- **Special Mechanics:** Increases health of ALL team members in the same dungeon team by 10%. This multiplier is applied after Strategy Room HP bonus and Class HP modifier. Not shown in pet stats, only visible in dungeon logs. Rogue class ability: +1.33% * CL item drop rate.
- **Stat Modifiers:** +30% Health, -30% Attack in dungeons
- **Synergies:** Benefits any team as a health buffer. Works with any dungeon team composition.
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** None
- **Token Improvement:** Further increases team health bonus by 0.03% per 250 strategy books given, limited by Dungeon Level. At DL 600 with 150k books, provides an extra 18% team health on top of the base 10%.
- **Constraint Implications:** Universal team health buffer (10% to all teammates). Stat profile is tank-oriented (+30% HP, -30% ATK). Token upgrade scales with strategy books given and DL, capping at 28% total team health bonus.

## Mermaid
- **Element:** Ice (evolution requires Magic Ice)
- **Special Mechanics:** Soul Extraction: every 3 turns, extracts soul from a random enemy (if no soul active and enemies without extracted souls exist). Soul appears as enemy with 25% HP of original, same stats and abilities. While soul exists, all pets gain +0.03% * CL speed per turn. After soul defeated, damages original enemy by 10% max HP, gives party shield of 0.5% * CL of max HP, and converts speed bonus to attack bonus until end of room. Soul extraction works even unevolved (but speed boost/shield won't apply at CL 0). Works on non-special monsters only (not bosses, Gram fights).
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented
- **Synergies:** Supporter class ability: +2% * CL heal power.
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** None
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Very complex dungeon mechanic with soul extraction. Provides speed boost, shield, and percent-based damage. Useful against high-HP enemies. Does not work on bosses.

## Honeybadger
- **Element:** Wind/Forest (evolution requires Magic Wood, consumes SSS+20 Jungle equipment set)
- **Special Mechanics:** Unstunnable (immune to stun). Assassin class ability: +2% * CL single target damage.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented
- **Synergies:** Works well with Hwangeum Pig in Forest D4 (if Hwangeum Pig is primary damage dealer, Honeybadger as Supporter is strong). Viable as lone Supporter in Forest D4 since being unstunnable prevents team wipes from Supporter being stunned.
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** Jungle/Forest element focus
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Stun immunity is unique and very valuable. Can be evolved as either Assassin or Supporter depending on team needs. As Supporter, stun immunity prevents the common Forest D4 team wipe scenario. As Assassin, may defeat Cosmic Gnomes without Basilisk if Strategy Room and Dojo are strong enough.

## Hamster
- **Element:** Not specified (unlocked in Forest dungeon)
- **Special Mechanics:** Only pet that can craft, upgrade, and reforge Earmuffs accessory (improves crafting speed for Blacksmith/Alchemist, maxed at +10% at SSS+20). Blacksmith specialist.
- **Stat Modifiers:** +30% Defense, -10% Attack, -10% Health, -10% Speed in dungeons
- **Synergies:** None documented for dungeons
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** None
- **Token Improvement:** Crafting quality/speed multiplied by 1 + 0.35% x CL. Receives half of additive crafting speed/quality bonuses.
- **Constraint Implications:** Stat modifiers make it defense-oriented but weak offensively. Primarily a crafting pet, not ideal for dungeon combat.

## Serow
- **Element:** Wind (evolution requires Bound Feather)
- **Special Mechanics:** Reduces resource cost of dungeon events and traps by 20 + DL/2, capped at 100% at DL 160. Items must be in inventory but won't be consumed. Does NOT work on Runes, Talismans, or Chocolate. Only works in Depths 1-3 (not D4, unless token improved). If team has both a trap-evading item and a Demonic Rune, the Rune is used and Serow cannot save it.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented
- **Synergies:** None documented
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** None
- **Token Improvement:** At cost of a pet token, Serow's ability works for ALL teams even when Serow is not in the dungeon, at 50% of current discount. ALSO extends ability to D4, at 25% of base value.
- **Constraint Implications:** Major resource-saving utility pet. Token improvement is very significant - applies to all teams passively. D1-D3 focused without token, D4 accessible with token at reduced rate.

## Ancient Mimic
- **Element:** Neutral by default, but element can be changed (evolution material changes with element)
- **Special Mechanics:** Can hit TWO targets at once in dungeons if equipped with Ele Twin Dagger (special weapon crafted from Shiny Element Stones dropped when Ancient Mimic lands killing blows on ancient mimic enemies). Assassin class ability: +2% * CL single target damage.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented
- **Synergies:** None documented
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** Ele Twin Dagger is a special weapon that enables dual-target attacks. Shiny Element Stones obtained from killing ancient mimic enemies with this pet.
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Dual-target attack with special weapon is unique. Element is changeable, which affects evolution material. Planner should note the Ele Twin Dagger as special equipment override.

## Student
- **Element:** Earth (follows formula 3.9*DL instead of standard 50+3*DL, meaning lower Earth below DL 55, higher after DL 56)
- **Special Mechanics:** Dungeon Alchemist - can earn Class XP in dungeons. Has a hidden upgrade: if 60 rooms of each D4-10 completed with DL 1000, CL 100 Student in party, Student upgrades to boost whole party's exp gain by 50% and increases Basilisk's attack by 50% if on same team. Bonus disabled if Student drops below DL 1000 or CL 100 but re-activates when thresholds are met again.
- **Stat Modifiers:** -10% penalty to ALL stats
- **Synergies:** Strong synergy with Basilisk (+50% attack to Basilisk when upgraded and on same team)
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** None
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Unique Earth element scaling formula. -10% all stats penalty. Hidden upgrade mechanic provides major party buff (+50% exp) and specific Basilisk synergy (+50% attack). Planner should flag Student+Basilisk as a synergy pair.

## Hwangeum Pig
- **Element:** Wind (gives pets in same party a 5 * CL bonus to wind element)
- **Special Mechanics:** Deals 15% max HP damage to an enemy every 3 turns (does not work in boss rooms, only as Assassin class). Provides wind element bonus to entire party (5 * CL). Assassin class ability: +2% * CL single target damage.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented
- **Synergies:** Designed to fight Ancient Mimics in Forest D4. Competes with Honeybadger and Todlicher Loffel as Forest D4 damage dealer. Works well with Honeybadger as Supporter.
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** Wind/Forest element focus
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Percent-based max HP damage (15% every 3 turns) is powerful against high-HP enemies like Ancient Mimics. Must be Assassin for this ability. Wind element party buff is unique and valuable for Forest teams. Boss room limitation is important.

## Nugget
- **Element:** Not specified
- **Special Mechanics:** Can freely change between classes once evolved. 0.3% * CL specialist bonus for each class. Counts for the correct class in dungeon events.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented
- **Synergies:** None documented
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** None
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Class flexibility is valuable for dungeon event coverage. Lower specialist bonus (0.3% vs standard) is the tradeoff. Similar to Nothing (Other) and Holy ITRTG Book.

## Arachne
- **Element:** Wind (evolution requires Magic Feather)
- **Special Mechanics:** Normal attacks have 0.7x damage modifier, but apply one stack of Poison per attack. Poison deals 0.003 * CL of Arachne's pure Attack stat per stack at end of round. If evolved as Mage, poison damage has 90% penalty. In boss rooms, poison damage reduced by 70%. If poisoned monster summons, the summon also gets equal poison stacks. Ability works on every class (not just Assassin).
- **Stat Modifiers:** 0.7x attack modifier on normal attacks (built-in penalty offset by poison)
- **Synergies:** None documented
- **Anti-synergies:** Poor as Mage (90% poison damage penalty) or Defender
- **Equipment Notes:** None
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Poison mechanic works on all classes except effectively penalized as Mage. 0.7x attack modifier means raw damage is lower but poison compensates over time. Boss room 70% reduction is significant. Flexible class choice except Mage/Defender.

## Pixie Goatmother
- **Element:** Wind (evolution requires SSS T4+20 Wind Equipment set, consumed)
- **Special Mechanics:** As Supporter, heals 30% less than normal BUT gets an extra action every turn. First heal action each turn has CL% chance to give the healed pet an extra action next turn at initiative 1. Does not work on Sniper.
- **Stat Modifiers:** -30% healing as Supporter (offset by extra action and high evo specialization bonus)
- **Synergies:** None documented specifically, but extra action grant benefits high-damage dealers
- **Anti-synergies:** Extra action grant does NOT work on Sniper
- **Equipment Notes:** Wind element focus. Evolution consumes SSS T4+20 Wind Equipment (does not need to be Enchanted). Pet token can bypass this.
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Unique Supporter with extra action mechanic. -30% heal penalty is significant but offset by double actions. CL% chance to grant extra action to healed pet is very powerful at high CL. Cannot grant extra action to Sniper. Wind element team specialist.

## Fainting Capra
- **Element:** Ice (evolution requires Magic Ice)
- **Special Mechanics:** Damage reduction: has chance to take less damage when attacked, equal to 10% + DL^0.6%. DR equals the proc chance, caps at 50%. After evolution becomes "Feinting Capra" - shreds defense and elements by (0.01 * CL)% on each attack if class is NOT Mage. Has built-in T4 knife effect. However, special effects of equipped knives are nullified. Rogue class ability: +2% * CL loot drop.
- **Stat Modifiers:** Damage reduction chance (10% + DL^0.6%, caps at 50%)
- **Synergies:** None documented
- **Anti-synergies:** Cannot benefit from equipped knife special effects (nullified)
- **Equipment Notes:** Knife special effects are nullified - do not rely on T4 knife effects for this pet. Built-in T4 knife effect compensates.
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Unique damage reduction mechanic scales with DL. Defense/element shred on every attack (non-Mage) is very powerful for team support. Knife equipment override: special effects nullified, so planner should not prioritize T4 knives for this pet. Built-in T4 knife effect means it works without special knife.

## Fool
- **Element:** Not specified
- **Special Mechanics:** Can confuse enemies it attacks in dungeons. Confuse chance formula: (((attacker elements/2) / defender elements) - 0.3)*100%. Needs more than 60% of enemy's elements for any chance. At 2600 elements vs 1000 enemy, 100% chance. Does NOT work as Mage. Defender class ability: +1.2% * CL damage taken instead of party member (max 50%), +10% protect chance.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented
- **Synergies:** None documented
- **Anti-synergies:** Confuse does not work as Mage class
- **Equipment Notes:** Element-stacking is important for confuse chance - prioritize total element value
- **Token Improvement:** Not documented
- **Constraint Implications:** Confuse mechanic is unique and element-dependent. Needs high total elements relative to enemies. Non-Mage class required for confuse. As Defender gets enhanced protect chance (+10%). Planner should note element priority for maximizing confuse.

## Nothing (Other)
- **Element:** Not specified
- **Special Mechanics:** Can freely change classes without loss or cost once evolved. Counts for correct class in dungeon events. 0.5% * CL bonus to all classes. Receives 30k base growth bonus after evolution. Campaign penalty of -75% becomes +75% after evolution.
- **Stat Modifiers:** None documented
- **Synergies:** None documented
- **Anti-synergies:** None documented
- **Equipment Notes:** None
- **Token Improvement:** Always counts as correct class for first required pet in Tavern quests. +0.15% * CL tavern quest CL experience.
- **Constraint Implications:** Free class-switching is valuable for flexibility. Higher specialist bonus (0.5% * CL) than Nugget (0.3% * CL). Secret pet with complex unlock/evo questline. Similar flexibility to Nugget and Holy ITRTG Book.
